IRON MAN

You think you are tough? You think you are smart? Buddy, Tony Stark built an entire armored supersuit in a cave. A CAVE. With a box of scraps. While you are still trying to figure out why your code will not center a button, he has already upgraded to Mark 85 and snapped half the universe out of existence. He is a billionaire, genius, playboy, philanthropist, and he did it all with a glowing car battery taped to his chest. You? You cannot even make your CSS file load. Iron Man did not just save the world. He saved the universe, cracked jokes while doing it, and made it look good. Meanwhile you sit there searching why your buttons are gone. Be more like Iron Man. Just do not be dead like Iron Man. That part is optional.
